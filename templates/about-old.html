About Me

Prioritas is an organizational website that enables users to keep track of to-dos and categorize these tasks to be completed in a priority matrix according to their level of importance and urgency. The website offers a Pomodoro feature through which users are able to optimize task completion by dividing work into manageable chunks of 25 minutes. Happy Prioritas-ing!

Built by Anya Zhang '20, Linda Qin '20, and Lena Episalla '19 for CS50. Questions? Comments? Please contact us at prioritas.CS50@gmail.com.
